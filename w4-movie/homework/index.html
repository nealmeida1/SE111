The Godfather

The Godfather is a 1972 film directed by Francis Ford Coppola, centered around Don Vito Corleone, the head of a powerful mafia family in New York. The story unfolds during his daughter's wedding, where his youngest son, Michael, a decorated WWII Marine, is present but initially uninterested in the family business. As the plot progresses, Michael becomes embroiled in the violent world of organized crime, ultimately taking over the family business after a series of betrayals and power struggles. The film explores themes of loyalty, power, and the complexities of family dynamics within the context of crime and morality.

Company Credits: Paramount Pictures, Albert S. Ruddy Productions, and Alfran Productions
Release Date: Mar 24, 1972
Genre: Crime/Drama
Rating: R
Running Time: 2h 55m

Nefarious

Nefarious is a horror thriller film about a convicted serial killer who claims he is a demon and predicts that the psychiatrist evaluating him will commit three murders. Set in Amsterdam, London, and the Teesdale Valley, the movie follows two dope-dealing outlaws on their quest to open a coffee shop in Amsterdam.

Company Credits: Believe Entertainment (II) and SDG Releasing
Release Date: April 14, 2023
Genre: Drama/Horror/Thriller
Rating: R
Running Time: 1h 37m
