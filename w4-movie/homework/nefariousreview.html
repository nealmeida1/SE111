NEFARIOUS REVIEW

REVIEW

Name: Nicholas Almeida

Due Date: 10/26/25


Nefarious is a fantastic thriller/horror and one of my favorite movies of all time. This movie will have you questioning everything the entire time you watch it. The buildup is fantastic and the ending will leave you speechless.

This movie is a must watch whether you are a horror fan or not. Sean Patrick Flannery gives a amazing performance. This movie is absolutely insane from a psychological standpoint. It is an eye opener when it comes to how demons operate and manipulate humans freedom.

Trivia

  • Sean Patrick Flanery explained in an interview that the script was so well-written that there was no improvisation from him, except for "Maybe two syllables."
  • It is a prequel to the novels 'A Nefarious Plot' (2016) and 'A Nefarious Carol' (2020) by Steve Deace.
  • Sean Patrick Flanery considers this film one of his top three, expressing pride in its authenticity and realism.
